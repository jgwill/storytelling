## The Core Technology Behind It

**NARINTEL is built on three systems:**

1. **NCP (Narrative Cognition Protocol)**
   - Standard way to represent stories with full metadata
   - Characters, themes, emotions, relationships all tracked
   - Enables cross-app compatibility

2. **Character Arc Tracking**
   - Every character's journey logged at every beat
   - Growth, relationships, and consistency maintained
   - Story makes sense when read aloud at any point

3. **Analytical Feedback Loop**
   - Constant assessment of emotional quality
   - Automatic enrichment of weak beats
   - Real-time suggestions for improvement
